Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Quarx AI handles data and respects your privacy.

Data

Most tools run entirely in your browser and do not send data to our servers. We do not require accounts. The only data stored locally is when you explicitly save notes (in your browser's localStorage).

Third-party services

We may include links to external services (e.g., public APIs). Those services are governed by their own policies.

Cookies & Analytics

We avoid invasive analytics by default. If analytics or ad services are added, they will be documented here and respect user privacy choices.
